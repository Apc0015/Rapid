How it works

From raw data to running agent
in three steps.

01
Connect your data
Point RAPID at your databases, document stores, and knowledge bases. It reads your schema, indexes your documents, and learns the data model — without copying a byte to external servers.
02
Train per department
Each department gets its own agent with scoped access — Finance sees financial tables, HR sees employee records. Role permissions are baked in at deploy time, not checked at query time.
03
Deploy & govern
Every query is logged. Every consequential action requires human approval. When agent confidence drops below your threshold, it escalates automatically to the right person — not just anyone.

Features

Built for enterprise-grade
AI deployment.

01
RBAC
Role-based intelligence — not just access
Every person in your organization sees exactly what they need, and nothing they shouldn't. Permissions are defined per role, per department, per table.
  • Per-table read / write / dept-only access tiers
  • Role propagation from HR system at login
  • Audit log for every query and action
02
Human always in the loop
No consequential AI action fires without a human sign-off. When confidence falls below your defined threshold, the agent escalates — to the right person, not just anyone.
  • Configurable confidence thresholds
  • Escalation chain maps to org hierarchy
  • Approval audit trail built in
03
Pluggable AI — your model, your infrastructure
RAPID runs on Anthropic, OpenAI, Azure OpenAI, or a local Ollama instance. Swap the model without rewriting a single agent definition.
  • Model-agnostic agent runtime
  • On-premise deployment with Ollama
  • No data leaves your perimeter
